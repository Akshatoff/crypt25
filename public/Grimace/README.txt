Grimace: A playful, slightly distorted sans-serif font. Its letters possess a hand-drawn quality, with uneven strokes and slightly wobbly lines, giving it a friendly, almost cartoonish feel.  The overall effect is informal and approachable, yet legible.  It suggests a sense of whimsy and fun.
